Mission

Our mission is to avoiding middleman who purchasing items for a much lower price from farmers and reselling then for higher price which great loss for the farmers.Customers are also buying the unhealthy products from market or other places which is stored 3 to 4 days after purchasing it from farmers, to avoid these problems we come up with an idea. We are making a connection between farmer and end customer who are passionate to buy fresh and healthy Vegetables and fruits with in two to three hours directly from farmers.We will provide door step delivery with no time to the end customer. Buyer can see vegetables & fruits in the farming fields through a live and do orders in the live itself according to their needs.
